My name is Joseph Nguyen, and I am a Web Developer who has a profound interest in programming.

I studied at UC Irvine for three years and that was when the Corona virus made all learning remote, which gave me the time to explore different things. I started to learn Python on Codecademy, and as my interest soared, so did my time investment in programming. I started to learn more on how to build apps on Udemy and afterwards I decided to take it to the next level and attend a full immersion Web Development program at LearningFuze.

I spent the summer right before my fourth year at UCI at LearningFuze and devoted 10-12 hours a day to learning and building projects. All of this experience lead to me being able to develop the skills needed to succeed in a work environment. I found this program very rewarding and I decided to to pursue this career field and accomplish the goals that I have with coding!
